🔔 Daily Fun Money Reminder

Set up a daily 10:00 AM lock screen notification using the built-in Reminders app. One time setup!

Setup steps:
1. Open the Reminders app on your iPhone
2. Tap ＋ New Reminder
3. Title it "Check your fun money balance 💸"
4. Tap the ⓘ info icon next to the reminder
5. Toggle on Date → set to today
6. Toggle on Time → set to 10:00 AM
7. Tap Repeat → choose Daily
8. Tap Done ✓

Every day at 10:00 AM a notification will appear on your lock screen. Tap it to open Reminders, then switch to your budget app!
